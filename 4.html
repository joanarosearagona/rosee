Chicken Hamonado

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken cut into serving pieces.
  • 1 8g MAGGI MAGIC SARAP.
  • 2 tbsp patis.
  • 1 tsp ground white pepper.
  • 1 tbsp calamansi juice.
  • cooking oil for deep-frying.
  • ½ cup chopped garlic.
  • 1 cup pineapple juice.
  • ¾ cup sugar.
  • salt to taste.
  • 1-2 cups grated cheddar cheese.

  • Description
  • Rub chicken with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP, patis, white pepper and calamansi juice. Leave to marinate for at least 15 minutes. Set aside.
  • Heat cooking oil. Fry marinated chicken until golden brown. Transfer on a paper lined plate to drain excess oil.
  • In the same pan, leave 2-3 tbsp of oil. Fry garlic until fragrant and golden brown. Add back prepared chicken.
  • Pour in pineapple juice. Add sugar and cook until sauce is almost dry. Season with salt to taste. Add grated cheese and cook until cheese melts.
