Chicken Ala King

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 kg whole chicken, chopped.
  • ¼ cup butter.
  • 1 cup button mushroom.
  • 1 8g MAGGI MAGIC SARAP.
  • 1 cup chicken stock.
  • ¾ cup evaporated milk.
  • ? cup NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream.
  • 1 egg yolk, slightly beaten.
  • ? cup sliced red bell pepper or pimientos.
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour or as needed.
  • 3 tbsp sherry wine (optional).

  • Description
  • Sauté chicken in butter until chicken turns white in color.
  • Stir in button mushroom then season with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP. Pour in chicken stock and boil for 10 minutes. Add evaporated milk, simmer without boiling for 3 minutes.
  • Add NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream and beaten egg. Stir in bell pepper or pimiento. Simmer over low heat for another 10 minutes.
  • Thicken with flour until desired consistency of the sauce is achieved.
  • Add sherry wine if desired. Serve hot.
