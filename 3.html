.Ihaw Ihaw Fiesta

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork liempo.
  • 1 kg chicken breast or thigh fillet.
  • Marinade:.
  • ½ cup MAGGI Savor, Classic, divided.
  • 2 8g MAGGI MAGIC SARAP.
  • ¼ cup minced garlic, divided.
  • juice of 10 calamansi, divided.
  • For Basting: Combine -.
  • 1 cup achuete oil.
  • 1 8g MAGGI MAGIC SARAP.
  • 1 tbsp sugar.
  • Green Mango and Bagoong Relish: Combine –.
  • 2 green mangoes, chopped.
  • 2 onions, chopped.
  • 3 tomatoes, seeded, chopped.
  • ¼ cup chopped wansuy.
  • ½ cup cooked bagoong alamang.
  • 1 tsp MAGGI MAGIC SARAP.

  • Description
  • Put pork and chicken in separate containers. Marinate in MAGGI Savor, MAGGI MAGIC SARAP, garlic and calamansi juice for at least 1 hour.
  • Grill marinated pork and chicken for 5-10 minutes while basting both sides occasionally with the prepared achuete oil baste.
  • Serve with the prepared Mango and Bagoong Relish on the side..
